At Aspeya, we strive to make a positive
difference to the lives of patients and consumers through science, insight, and innovation.

We are on a journey to empower healthier lifestyles and target specific unmet needs. We do this by being innovative, optimizing formulas, and applying rigorous scientific standards.

Who we are

We’re harnessing deep insight and scientific expertise to reimagine how ingredients can be used, and working towards the development and commercialization of a pipeline of wellness and healthcare products and brands.

Our team

Aspeya operates as a separate company under the ownership of Philip Morris International Inc., and benefits from access to the oral technology platform of Fertin Pharma, a contract development and manufacturing organization (CDMO) that is a wholly owned subsidiary of Aspeya. Fertin Pharma develops and manufactures innovative oral and intra-oral delivery systems.

“I’m proud of the energy and expertise that our teams across the world bring in our pursuit of a single overarching principle: ‘responsibility by design’. Sourcing, innovating, robust scientific evidence, product development, regulatory compliance, adequate commercial and medical activities, all guided by the quest to serve unmet patient and consumer needs.”

MICHAEL KUNST
CEO, Aspeya
