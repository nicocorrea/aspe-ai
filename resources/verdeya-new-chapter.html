Lausanne, Switzerland

We are proud to introduce a new name in health and wellbeing cannabinoid products. Verdeya.

With a patient and consumer-first mindset, Verdeya has an ambition to address unmet needs with best-in-class cannabinoid products and safe and effective delivery systems.

Verdeya is committed to investing in cannabinoid science, and working with others to create consistent, evidence-based, high standards for cannabinoid quality, safety, and predictability. Its goal is to develop innovative and scientifically substantiated medical and non-recreational products with enhanced formulations for targeted populations and effective outcomes.

Julia Hoeng, VP, Innovation and Product Development (Cannabinoids), said: “We believe that cannabinoids have a role to play in addressing health and wellbeing needs. But there is a need for science-backed products and standards for safety and quality.

“We are pioneering a responsible, scientific, and long-term approach to the research, development, and commercialization of medical and non-recreational cannabinoid products, in accordance with applicable regulations.”

Verdeya is a wholly owned subsidiary of Aspeya, Inc. It functions as an independent legal and operational entity with its own governance structure. To learn more about Verdeya, visit the website at verdeyalife.com.

About Verdeya

Verdeya is pioneering a scientific and responsible, long-term approach to cannabinoids, harnessing nature to address unmet medical and wellbeing needs. With a patient and consumer-first mindset, we develop and commercialize best-in-class medical and non-recreational cannabinoid products. We are doing this by combining scientific strength, world-class technical expertise, and data-driven insights. Verdeya is committed to working with others to create consistent, high standards for cannabinoid quality, safety, and predictability. Our people are dedicated, experienced scientists and industry experts, all passionate about unlocking the medical and wellbeing potential of cannabinoids to improve patient and consumer lives. Verdeya is a wholly owned subsidiary of Aspeya, Inc. It functions as an independent legal and operational entity with its own governance structure. Verdeya maintains full accountability for its strategic direction, operational execution, and financial stewardship.
